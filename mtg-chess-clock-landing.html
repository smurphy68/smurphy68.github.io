MTG Chess Clock

This aim of this project was to help me and my friends play more games of Magic: The Gathering without worrying about spending hours on one game. If your time is up, you lose...

  • JavaScript

  • CSS3

  • HTML5

Project Break Down

The MTG chess clock consists of 4 timers and life totals. The turn can be passed sequentially clockwise around the table. It is intended to operate on a tablet in the middle of the table. The toggle switch allows any player to be the first in turn order.
